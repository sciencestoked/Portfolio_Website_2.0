BeingArtifex, IIT(ISM) Dhanbad: Mentoring the Next Generation

At BeingArtifex, IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, I mentored over 1000 students, covering a wide range of topics including Python Programming, Arduino, IoT, C++ Programming, and various design and management skills. BeingArtifex is a student-driven community dedicated to fostering technical and leadership skills.

One of our flagship initiatives was Skillathon, designed to introduce school students to technology. As the event coordinator for the second edition, I ensured the event's success by overseeing its smooth execution.

I also conducted numerous talks and seminars on robotics and machine learning, aimed at equipping students with the knowledge and skills to excel in these fields.

My role involved teaching essential programming languages and tools, and providing insights into public speaking and graphic design. This experience allowed me to contribute significantly to the development of future technologists and leaders.

BeingArtifex played a crucial role in honing my leadership skills and deepening my knowledge across multiple technical domains.
