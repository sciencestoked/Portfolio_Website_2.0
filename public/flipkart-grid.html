Flipkart GRiD 4.0: Innovating E-Commerce with Blockchain

In the Flipkart GRiD 4.0 competition, I developed a blockchain-based e-commerce warranty system using NFTs. This project aimed to provide a secure and transparent way to handle product warranties and customer claims.

Leveraging my knowledge of blockchain technology and Ethereum smart contracts, I created a system that enhances trust and reliability in e-commerce transactions. The system enables users to verify the authenticity of their product warranties and streamline the warranty claim process.

Being a national semi-finalist in this competition was a significant milestone, demonstrating my ability to apply cutting-edge technologies to solve real-world problems.
