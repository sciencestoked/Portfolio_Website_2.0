BeingArtifex, IIT(ISM) Dhanbad: Mentoring the Next Generation

As a mentor at BeingArtifex, IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, I had the opportunity to guide and educate over 1000 students. BeingArtifex is a student learning community that focuses on fostering technical and leadership skills among students. My mentorship covered a broad range of topics including Python Programming, Arduino, IoT, C++ Programming, and various design and management skills.

One of our notable initiatives was Skillathon, an event designed to introduce school students to the world of technology early on. In the second edition of Skillathon, I served as the event coordinator, ensuring the program ran smoothly and effectively.

Beyond Skillathon, I also delivered numerous talks and seminars at our college, educating fellow students about robotics and machine learning. These sessions aimed to inspire and equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in these fields.

My role at BeingArtifex involved teaching essential programming languages and tools, as well as providing insights into public speaking and graphic design. This experience was fulfilling as it allowed me to contribute to the development of aspiring technologists and leaders.

BeingArtifex was instrumental in honing my leadership skills and deepening my knowledge across multiple technical domains.
