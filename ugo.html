1. Ugo Inc - Online Internship

During my online internship at Ugo Inc, I focused on optimizing robotic systems through advanced machine learning techniques. A major achievement was enhancing the performance of the elevator button detection system by an impressive 3X. This was accomplished by employing data augmentation and model optimization, which involved:

  • Data Augmentation: Utilizing various techniques to artificially expand the training dataset, thereby improving model robustness and accuracy.
  • Model Optimization: Fine-tuning the existing models to achieve better performance metrics and efficiency.

Additionally, I undertook the conversion of YOLO models to ONNX format for deployment on Jetson Nano, enhancing model performance and compatibility with edge devices. I also implemented RMF Core and task dispatcher mechanisms for collective robot control using ROS 2 Humble and Gazebo Fortress/Ignition, which streamlined multi-robot coordination and task management.
